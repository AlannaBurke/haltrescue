# So You Want to Run a Rescue?

Starting an animal rescue is a noble and rewarding endeavor, but it is also a significant undertaking that requires immense dedication, resources, and emotional resilience. It is far more than simply saving animals; it involves running a complex, non-profit business. This series will guide you through the fundamental pillars of starting and sustaining a successful animal rescue organization.



### Key Areas Covered in this Series:

*   **[Getting Started](./getting-started):** The foundational steps, from defining your mission to navigating the legal requirements of establishing a 501(c)(3) nonprofit.
*   **[Getting Supplies](./getting-supplies):** Strategies for sourcing the essential supplies needed to care for your animals, from food and housing to medical necessities.
*   **[Establishing Your Network](./establish-your-network):** The critical importance of building a strong network of volunteers, foster homes, veterinary partners, and community supporters.
*   **[Leveling Up Your Skills](./level-up-your-skills):** Moving beyond the basics to develop the advanced skills in animal care, fundraising, and management needed for long-term success.
